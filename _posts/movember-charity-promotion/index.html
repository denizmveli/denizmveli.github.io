Movember Charity Promotion

December 13, 2011

Ladies and Gentlemen,/o:p

The Modern Ink is pleased to announce a new promotion running for the entire month of Movember (formally November)./o:p

For this month, all profit made from the sale of our app, Vodafone Usage Plus, will be donated to the Movember charity. /o:p

If you are unaware, Movember is a month long event held around the world where men don the most hideous of facial accessories, a Moustache, with the sole aim of raising vital funds and awareness for men’s health issues, specifically prostate cancer and depression in men./o:p

For more information or if you’d like to make a larger contribution to Movember, please head over to www.movember.com./o:p
