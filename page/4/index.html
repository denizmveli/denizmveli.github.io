The AdGives Experiment

December 13, 2011
Tonight we are pleased to announce the launch of our new project AdGives. AdGives is online advertising mixed with social activism. Part of the profit from every ad click is donated to charity. It’s all going to kick off soon with a trial we’re calling The AdGives Experiment. The site explains it best, so rather than repeating myself why don’t you check it out? www.TheAdGivesExperiment.com The goal is big. To change the business model of online advertising so that the millions spent can be used to give back to useful real-world causes, while still achieving the advertising objectives of all parties involved.
