Vodafone Usage Plus Available

December 13, 2011

A new version of Vodafone Usage is available on the Android market which brings about a number of exciting changes.

The app is now available in two editions.

  • Vodafone Usage Plus
    The featured packed version, with new configurable 1x1 & 1x2 widgets, no ads, and more to come. Available for purchase through the market for just $0.99.

  • Vodafone Usage
    The advertising supported free edition previously available on the market.

What’s in Plus?
_
_The Plus version gives users the customisations and enhancements they want to make the application just that little bit more personalised. For just under a buck you will have an advertising free version of the app that will always have the latest features.

In this version there are new advanced widgets including a 1x1 widget, to help save that precious home screen space. All Plus widgets now have an options screen where users can select the bundle type they wish to display on the widget. You can also choose whether you would like to see the quota amount remaining or the amount used. Future releases will allow the user to customise the look and feel of the widget.

The widgets also display the days left in the month which should be handy when it’s coming towards the end of the month.
