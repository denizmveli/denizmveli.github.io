The Modern Ink worked with the Guvera Mobile team to create a feature rich Android presence for it’s existing online music streaming service.

The app brings Guvera users unlimited free streaming music while giving them the freedom to take their music anywhere, on or offline. User’s can search and create unlimited playlist and view recommended tracks. The UX stays true to the Guvera brand while delivering a truly native Android look and feel.
