Say hello to Jive

August 18, 2012

UPDATE: Jive is no more. Our renamed app Hooha is now available on Google Play.

Say hello to Jive, a brand new Android app for the brand new platform App.net.

This is a very raw alpha build so please be aware the app is in no way complete. Then again neither is App.net so lets just roll with it.

Things Jive can do:

  • View your stream, mentions and global posts.

  • Compose a new post.

  • Reply or repost other posts.

  • Open links from posts.

  • Android 2.2 +

Things Jive will have soon:

  • User handling - @user tags will open in your mobile browser at the moment.

  • Hashtag handling - #hashtags will open in your mobile browser at the moment.

  • Better refresh - refresh blows away the stream and loads it again. It needs to be smarter.

  • Background loading.

  • More and more awesome features.

If you have access to App.net alpha please try out Jive and let me know what you think. If not http://join.app.net

There’s an option in-app to send feedback. I’d really appreciate it.

UPDATE : v 0.2.0

  • Share links/text from other Android apps to Jive.

  • Tab select scroll to top.

  • Drop down options to reply and repost from stream.

  • Fixed sort order of posts.

  • Display usernames instead of full names.
